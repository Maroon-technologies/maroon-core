## Core Products & Systems

### 1. Truth Teller Engine

**Patent Status**: Provisional filed, utility pending  
**Tech Stack**: Vertex AI, Gemini 1.5 Pro, BigQuery, Cloud Spanner  
**Current State**: R&D phase (70% complete)

**Architecture**:

```
Signal Ingestion → Pattern Recognition → Integrity Scoring → Prediction Output
     ↓                    ↓                      ↓                    ↓
 External APIs      Gemini 1.5 Pro        Custom Algorithm       BigQuery
 (Zillow, WSDA)     Pattern Matching      (0.0-1.0 scale)      (Persistence)
```

**Core Innovation**:

- Every prediction gets an **integrity score** (0.0 = low confidence, 1.0 = high confidence)
- Traceable back to specific regulatory signals
- Self-correcting based on outcome validation

**Staffing Needs**:

- Lead AI Engineer (owns architecture)
- 2x ML Engineers (model training, tuning)
- Data Engineer (corpus pipeline)
- Research Analyst (signal validation)

**Revenue Model**:

- SaaS subscription: $5k-$50k/month enterprise
- API access: $0.10 per prediction call
- Licensing to Maroon entities: Internal transfer pricing
