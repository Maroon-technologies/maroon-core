## Operational Playbooks

### Patent Prosecution Workflow

1. **Invention Disclosure**: Maroon entity submits innovation
2. **Prior Art Search**: Patent agent conducts USPTO/Google Patents search
3. **Patentability Opinion**: Managing Partner assesses novelty
4. **Provisional Filing**: Immediate protection (if viable)
5. **Utility Conversion**: 9-12 months after provisional (claims refinement)
6. **Prosecution**: Respond to USPTO office actions
7. **Issuance → Maintenance**: 4-year, 8-year, 12-year fees

**SLA**: Provisional filed within 30 days of disclosure

---

### WIC/EBT Certification Support

1. **Application Prep**: Senior Associate drafts state application
2. **Compliance Review**: Verify food catalog, health codes
3. **Submission**: File with WA DSHS (or state agency)
4. **Follow-up**: Coordinate inspections, respond to questions
5. **Approval**: Typically 6-8 weeks
6. **Maintenance**: Annual recertification support

**Owner**: Senior Associate

---

### Regulatory Monitoring (Law Firm OS)

1. **Daily**: Truth Teller scans FDA, USDA, USPTO updates
2. **Analysis**: AI flags relevant changes for Maroon entities
3. **Alert**: Automated email/Slack to affected entity
4. **Guidance**: Senior Associate provides compliance roadmap
5. **Follow-up**: Monthly regulatory brief to all entities

**Owner**: Managing Partner + Truth Teller integration
