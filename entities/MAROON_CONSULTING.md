## Core Services & Offerings

### 1. Operational Ontology Design

**Description**: Help enterprises build hierarchical meaning structures (Signal → Pattern → Protocol → Ontology) for defensible decision-making.

**Deliverables**:

- **Ontology Audit**: 2-week assessment of current systems and meaning structures
- **Framework Design**: Custom 4-level ontology for client's domain
- **Playbook**: Operational protocols and SOPs aligned with ontology
- **Training**: Executive and team workshops on implementation

**Ideal Clients**:

- FinTech companies (payment systems, compliance)
- HealthTech (regulatory-heavy, patient data)
- GovTech (municipal systems, transparency)
- Food/Ag (supply chain, traceability)

**Pricing**: $150k-$500k per engagement (12-16 weeks)

**Case Study - Target Client**:

- **Company**: Regional grocery chain (Pacific Northwest)
- **Problem**: Fragmented WIC/EBT compliance across 50 stores
- **Solution**: Maroon ontology + automated compliance monitoring (powered by Truth Teller)
- **Result**: 90% reduction in compliance violations, $2M annual savings
- **Revenue**: $350k engagement + $50k/year maintenance

## Operational Playbooks

### Client Engagement Process

1. **Discovery**: Qualify need, budget authority, timeline (BANT)
2. **Proposal**: Custom SOW, deliverables, pricing
3. **Kickoff**: Workshop with client stakeholders (Week 1)
4. **Execution**: Weekly sprints, deliverables every 2 weeks
5. **Closeout**: Final presentation, playbook handoff, retrospective
6. **Follow-up**: 30-60-90 day check-ins, upsell opportunities

**Project Manager**: Principal Consultant (owns client relationship)

### Ontology Design Framework

**Week 1-2**: Current state assessment

- Stakeholder interviews
- System audit
- Pain point mapping

- Protocol creation (SOPs and playbooks)
- Governance rules (ontology layer)

**Week 7-10**: Implementation planning

- Roadmap creation
- Change management strategy
- Training curriculum design

- Executive presentation
- Team workshops
