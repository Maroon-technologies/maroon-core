### 3. EBT Split Transaction Apparatus (Patent)

**Patent Status**: Utility application filed  
**CPC Codes**: G06Q 20/00 (Payment architectures), G06Q 30/00 (Commerce)  
**Commercial Value**: $5M-$50M

**Innovation**:
Traditional EBT systems can't split transactions between WIC-eligible and non-eligible items in real-time. Our patented system:

1. Scans cart items against WIC database (real-time)
2. Calculates eligible vs. non-eligible split
3. Routes WIC payment to state processor
4. Routes remainder to credit/debit processor
5. Generates compliant receipt for both transactions

**Time**: <200ms average split calculation  
**Compliance**: USDA-approved, state-certified (WA, AK pending)

**Licensing Potential**:

- Target: National grocery chains (Safeway, Kroger, Albertsons)
- License fee: $0.10-$0.25 per EBT transaction
- Market size: 42M SNAP households nationally
- Revenue potential: $50M-$200M/year (if widely adopted)

**Staffing Needs**:

- Director of Technology (owns implementation)
- 2x FinTech Engineers (AWS Lambda, payment processing)
- Compliance specialist (USDA certification)

## Operational Playbooks

### Commissary Onboarding

1. **Application**: Micro-business applies online
2. **Vetting**: Health code compliance check, background
3. **Training**: Food safety, WIC/EBT certification
4. **Lease**: Month-to-month agreement ($20-$30/sq ft)
5. **Launch**: Marketing support, Onitas Market integration

**Timeline**: 4-6 weeks from application to launch

### WIC/EBT Certification Process

1. **State application**: Apply to WA DSHS (or state agency)
2. **Inspection**: Health code + WIC catalog compliance
3. **Approval**: 6-8 weeks
4. **Integration**: EBT split system deployment
5. **Training**: Staff + tenants on compliance

**Owner**: Director of Compliance

### Delivery Optimization

1. **Zone mapping**: Divide service area into delivery zones
2. **Route optimization**: ShipDay AI routing
3. **Peak hours**: 5-8 PM (dinner), 11 AM-2 PM (lunch)
4. **Driver coordination**: 2-3 drivers per zone during peak

**Owner**: Delivery Coordinator
